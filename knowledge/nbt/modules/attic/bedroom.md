# Attic module — Bedroom loft

> Module guide for the **bedroom attic**. It loads into the system prompt **only when the
> user picks the Bedroom attic** (or a structure that selects it). An attic lives in the
> roof void, so the build MUST have a pitched roof (never a flat one).

## What a bedroom attic is

The space **under the pitched roof**, finished as a **cosy upstairs bedroom** tucked into
the gable — a bed under the slope, a rug, a side table and a lamp. Reached by a **ladder**
from the top floor (the structure cuts the hatch + ladder for you).

## How to build it

1. **Finished floor.** A solid floor across the loft at the wall-top level in proper
   **planks/carpet** — it's a living space, not a store.
2. **Place the bed under the headroom.** Set the **bed** where the roof is tallest (along
   the ridge) so a player can stand beside it; tuck low furniture under the slopes.
3. **Keep the ladder + hatch clear** (the structure placed them) so you can climb out.
4. **Light + dress it.** A lantern or lamp, a **rug**, a bedside table (slab/barrel) with a
   light, maybe a small **gable window** for daylight, a chest at the foot of the bed.

## Avoid

- Leaving it a bare rough store (that's the Storage attic).
- A second roof or raised walls — the room IS the existing roof void.
- A bed jammed under the lowest slope with no standing room beside it.
