# Attic module — Storage loft

> Module guide for the **storage attic**. It loads into the system prompt **only when the
> user picks the Storage attic** (or a structure that selects it). An attic lives in the
> roof void, so the build MUST have a pitched roof (never a flat one).

## What a storage attic is

The triangular space **under the pitched roof**, floored over as a **rough utility loft**
for chests, barrels and clutter — not a living room. Reached by a **ladder** from the top
floor (the structure cuts the hatch + ladder for you).

## How to build it

1. **Floor the void.** A solid floor across the loft at the wall-top level — plain boards
   or the plinth/foundation material, not fine planks.
2. **Keep the ladder + hatch clear.** Don't block the ladder column or the step-off hole in
   the loft floor (the structure placed them) — you climb out there.
3. **Light it.** A standing lantern / torch on the floor (the sloped roof above has no flat
   ceiling to hang from).
4. **Furnish as storage:** rows of **chests/barrels**, a couple of **item frames**, hay
   bales, a few cobwebs in the corners — a believable attic store.

## Avoid

- Treating it as a bedroom/living space (that's the Bedroom attic).
- A second roof, or raising the walls — the loft IS the existing roof void.
- Blocking the ladder/hatch, or leaving it pitch-dark.
