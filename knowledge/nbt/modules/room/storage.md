# Room module — Storage room

> Furniture vocabulary for this room. Scale, preset, and decoration come from the
> `[Room plan]` brief + `14-furnishing-by-space.md`; here is just WHAT to build.

A storage room is the household's utilitarian back room / pantry — full but ORGANISED, so
it reads as deliberate storage, not clutter. Stack things against the walls and keep a
clear path through the middle.

**Walls of storage:**
- Runs of **barrels** and **chests** stacked 2–3 high against the walls (the staple).
- **Shelving**: slabs mounted on the wall with **item frames** above showing what's stored
  (food, tools, ingots), or `chiseled_bookshelf` for ledgers.
- **Sacks & crates**: `composter`s (open sacks of grain/produce), more barrels as crates,
  the odd `cauldron` (a bin/water).

**Hanging & standing:**
- **Hanging tools**: item frames with axes/picks/shovels, a `grindstone` or `stonecutter`
  in a corner if it doubles as a utility room.
- Stacked **bundles/kegs**: columns of `hay_block`, `barrel`, or `decorated_pot`.
- A small worktop (slab + barrel base) for sorting.

**Light:** a single working **lantern** (or two) — enough to see by; a storeroom doesn't
need to be bright or pretty. Below-grade openings use `iron_bars`, never glass. Floor in
plain `*_planks`, `cobblestone`, or `stone`.
