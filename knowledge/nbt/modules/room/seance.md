# Room module — Séance Parlour

> Furniture vocabulary for this room. Scale, preset, and decoration come from the
> `[Room plan]` brief + `14-furnishing-by-space.md`; here is just WHAT to build.

A séance parlour is a Victorian room for contacting the dead — genteel furnishings turned
uncanny. The defining move is a **round table set under a low candelabrum**, walled in by
**heavy drapes** and **cabinets of curiosities**.

**The séance table (centre):**
- A **round table** (a `*_fence`/`fence`-leg + slab/pressure-plate top, or a ring of stairs
  facing in around a centre block) with **chairs** all around (stairs + trapdoor arms).
- At its heart: a cluster of **candles**, and a divining centrepiece — a **crystal-gazing**
  orb (an `end_rod`/`glass`/`sea_lantern` under glass) or a **spirit board** inlaid on the
  tabletop (contrasting carpet/concrete glyphs).

**Light over the table:**
- A low **hanging candelabrum** directly above — `lantern`s/candles on a `chain` + `fence`
  frame — casting a single pool of light over the sitting. Keep the rest of the room dim.

**The uncanny décor:**
- **Cabinets of curiosities** along the walls: glass cases (`glass`/`tinted_glass` over
  shelves) of jars, **bones**/`skull`s, relics and oddities in `item_frame`s.
- **Grimoire shelves** (`bookshelf`/`chiseled_bookshelf`), a **mounted taxidermy** piece (a
  mob head, antlers from `chain`/`end_rod`), and **portraits** (item-framed maps/paintings).
- **Heavy drapes**: floor-length `wool`/banner curtains over every window; a large patterned
  **rug** (bordered carpet) under the table.

**A receiving lounge:** a **fireplace** (`campfire`/`magma` in a brick surround) with **wing
chairs** (stairs + trapdoor + banner back), a chaise, and a side table with a decanter.

**Scale up:** add a curtained **crystal-gazing alcove** (a draped side table with a glowing
orb and a fortune-teller's chair), tall **draped mirrors**, and a `chiseled_bookshelf` wall
with a ladder.

**Light:** dim and warm-but-eerie — the candelabrum pool plus a few sconces; never bright.
Dark `*_planks` or polished-stone floor, patterned rugs, wood-panelled or papered walls.
