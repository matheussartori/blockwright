# Room module — Ritual Chamber

> Furniture vocabulary for this room. Scale, preset, and decoration come from the
> `[Room plan]` brief + `14-furnishing-by-space.md`; here is just WHAT to build.

A ritual chamber is a cult's summoning room. The defining move is a **circle scribed
into the floor** with an **altar** at its head and **candlelight** doing the work — the
overhead light stays low so the flames carry the dread.

**The binding circle (floor):**
- Inlay a **circle/sigil** into the floor with a contrasting material — `crimson`/`warped`
  or coloured `wool`/`carpet`/`concrete` against the stone, or polished-stone rings. For a
  pentagram, run straight `chain`/slab lines between points.
- Ring it with **candles** (single and clustered, lit) on the floor or on short pillars
  (`*_wall` posts, `cobbled_deepslate_wall` + candle).

**The altar:**
- A raised **stone block** (`smooth_stone`/`polished_blackstone`/`deepslate`) draped with a
  carpet/banner, set at the head of the circle.
- On top: a **candelabrum** (candles, `sea_lantern` hidden under), a chained tome
  (`lectern` or a `chiseled_bookshelf`), a `skull`/`wither_skeleton_skull`, a brewing stand.
- Above it, a hung **idol or relic** — a mob head, a banner sigil, an item frame.

**Atmosphere pieces:**
- **Braziers** in the corners: fire in a `cauldron`/`iron` basin, or a `campfire` ringed by
  `deepslate` — low, flickering light.
- A **reagent wall**: shelves (`barrel`, `chiseled_bookshelf`) of bottles (`*_potion` in
  frames, `glass`/`tinted_glass` jars), hanging dried `vine`/`hanging_roots`, a caged
  specimen (`iron_bars` cage with a skull).
- Bigger rooms add **pews** facing the altar (stair+slab benches split by a central aisle),
  flanking **pillars** hung with `chain`s and banners, and a side **sacrificial slab** or
  iron **hanging cage** (`iron_bars`).

**Light:** keep it DARK. A few cold/sparse sources (`soul_lantern`) plus the candle and
brazier glow — islands of light in gloom, never an evenly-lit room. Floors in `deepslate`,
`blackstone`, or `polished` dark stone.
