# Surroundings module — Modern grounds

> Module guide for the **modern** surroundings ring. It loads into the system prompt
> **only when the user picks the Modern surroundings**. The ring is CODE-BUILT and LOCKED
> (part of the seeded shell): the yard already exists when you start — your job is to
> ENRICH it with outdoor detail, never to rebuild or cover it.

## What is already there (do not remove or cover)

The build box is LARGER than the house: the outer margin all around is the yard — it
SCALES with the house (a bigger villa gets wider grounds) and its corners are cut by
seeded chamfers, so the footprint is never a plain rectangle. Everything is laid at
ground level (y=0 of the box, or the ground-floor level over a basement podium):

- A **white-quartz pool terrace** across the front, with a **recessed water pool**
  (1 deep, flush with the deck) and sea-lantern lights embedded at its corners.
- A **stepped entry walk** aligned with the front door: quartz stair threshold at the
  street edge, dark polished-blackstone edging with in-floor sea lanterns, leading
  straight to the door.
- **White-concrete planters** with clipped shrubs flanking the walk and the house front.
- A **manicured hedge** (persistent leaves, 1 high) around the whole yard perimeter,
  gapped at the entry walk.
- **Lawns** on the sides and back with scattered bushes and light bollards.

## What to add (outdoor detail only)

- **Pool furniture**: loungers from quartz **stairs/slabs** beside the pool, a small
  side table (slab on a fence post), an umbrella (banner/fence-and-slab parasol).
- **Planting**: more potted plants (flower pots on full blocks), azalea/leaf bushes on
  the lawns, a slim feature tree in a back corner (log trunk + persistent leaves).
- **Lighting**: extra sea lanterns embedded in the terrace, or low bollards on lawns.
- **Terrace detail**: a fire-pit cluster (campfire ringed by stairs as seats) on the
  back or side lawn, stepping-stone slabs across grass.

## Rules

- **Everything outdoors stays LOW** (≤ 2–3 blocks above ground) and OPEN-AIR — never
  roof, wall in, or floor over any part of the yard ring.
- **Never fill or drain the pool**, never deck over the water.
- **Keep the entry walk clear** end to end — nothing placed on the path cells.
- **Leaves must be `persistent: true`** (placed foliage decays in-game otherwise).
- The house's interior/facade work follows the structure's own guide — this guide only
  governs the ring OUTSIDE the walls.
