# Surroundings module — Modern grounds

> Module guide for the **modern** surroundings ring. It loads into the system prompt
> **only when the user picks the Modern surroundings**. The ring is CODE-BUILT and LOCKED
> (part of the seeded shell): the yard already exists when you start — your job is to
> ENRICH it with outdoor detail, never to rebuild or cover it.

## What is already there (do not remove or cover)

The build box is LARGER than the house: the outer margin all around is the yard — it
SCALES with the house (a bigger villa gets a bigger, DENSER plot). This is the most
CONSTRUCTED of the yards, and it is rectilinear (crisp rectangles, no chamfered corners).
Everything is laid at ground level (y=0 of the box, or the ground-floor level over a
basement podium) and stays ≤3 cells tall:

- A **modern perimeter wall**: white-concrete **pillars** (2 tall) at every corner and on
  a regular rhythm, each capped with a **lantern** or a quartz slab, linked by **dark
  iron-bar panels** (2 tall). The front run is opened at the door column for a **gate**
  flanked by taller lantern piers.
- A **quartz pool terrace** across the front, with a **recessed water pool** (1 deep, flush
  with the deck), a **white-concrete coping frame**, sea-lantern **edge lights**, and a pair
  of quartz-stair **sun loungers** on the house side.
- A striped **entry walk** aligned with the front door: a quartz stair threshold at the gate
  mouth, a quartz spine flanked by light-grey tiles with quartz cross-rungs, plus in-ground
  sea lanterns, leading straight to the door.
- Raised **lilac beds** (grass cut into the terrace) flanking the walk.
- Quartz **side patios** wrapping the house and a one-cell **light-grey paving band** framing
  the plot just inside the wall.
- A few crisp **topiary** columns (3-tall persistent leaves) at the terrace/pool corners, and
  light bollards. **Clean lawn** on the sides/back — manicured, NOT scattered with bushes.

This yard is deliberately TIDY and hard-landscaped (see the references): clean lawn + paving,
defined beds, a few topiary accents. Do **not** scatter loose bushes/leaves across the grass
— no "weedy" look.

## What to add (outdoor detail only)

- **Pool furniture**: loungers from quartz **stairs/slabs** beside the pool, a small
  side table (slab on a fence post), an umbrella (banner/fence-and-slab parasol).
- **Planting (restrained + tidy)**: a FEW potted plants (flower pots on full blocks) on the
  terrace, or a single slim feature tree in a back corner — never loose bushes scattered over
  the lawn. Keep greenery to defined beds/planters and the existing topiary.
- **Lighting**: extra sea lanterns embedded in the terrace, or low bollards on the patio.
- **Terrace detail**: a fire-pit cluster (campfire ringed by stairs as seats) on a side
  patio, stepping-stone slabs across grass.

## Rules

- **Everything outdoors stays LOW** (≤ 2–3 blocks above ground) and OPEN-AIR — never
  roof, wall in, or floor over any part of the yard ring.
- **Never fill or drain the pool**, never deck over the water.
- **Keep the entry walk clear** end to end — nothing placed on the path cells.
- **Leaves must be `persistent: true`** (placed foliage decays in-game otherwise).
- The house's interior/facade work follows the structure's own guide — this guide only
  governs the ring OUTSIDE the walls.
