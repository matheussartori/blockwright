# Exterior module — Sakura house

> Module guide for the **sakura** exterior style. It loads into the system prompt **only
> when the user picks the Sakura exterior**. An exterior style is the house's OUTSIDE
> finish — cladding, trim, roof colour, window treatment — plus a couple of signature
> added volumes. Build it yourself with ordinary ops on top of `05-building-houses.md`.

## The look

A **cherry-blossom cottage**: soft **pink cherry-wood cladding** on a **stone base**, a
**dark slate roof**, and **pink blossoms** (cherry-leaf foliage) spilling over the eaves
and cascading down the corners. Leafy, romantic, springtime — a house in a grove of
cherry trees. Note the reference has an **external front stair** climbing to the upper
floor and a **covered upper terrace** — build those (you own the footprint).

## Materials (skin)

- **Walls:** `cherry_planks` (pink) over a `cobblestone` / `stone_bricks` lower storey.
- **Corners / posts:** `cherry_log` and `stripped_cherry_log` beams.
- **Roof:** `deepslate_tile_stairs` (dark grey), steep, with a **1-block overhang**.
- **Windows:** `glass_pane`, often with **`cherry_leaves` window boxes** (a leaf/flower
  block on a slab sill under each window).
- **Door / rails:** `cherry_door`, `cherry_fence` balustrades.
- **Blossom:** `cherry_leaves` (foliage) — the signature material.
- **Light:** `lantern` (often hung from the eaves on chains).

## Signature volumes

1. **Blossom crown.** Drape `cherry_leaves` along the **front eave** and **cascade them
   down the corners** toward the ground, so the roof reads "crowned with blossom" and
   boughs frame the facade.
2. **External front stair.** A `cherry_stairs` / slab flight climbing **outside** the
   house up to a **second-storey door** — a stone-and-wood stairway with a fence rail.
3. **Covered upper terrace.** A railed (`cherry_fence`) **balcony on the upper floor**,
   roofed by an overhang, with a planter or two (`cherry_leaves` on slabs) and a lantern.
4. **Window boxes.** A leaf/flower block on a slab sill under the front windows.

## Avoid

- A flat, leafless box — the **blossoms + the external stair + the covered terrace** are
  the whole identity; include all three.
- A bright/red roof — keep it **dark slate** so the pink + blossom pop against it.
