# Exterior module — Gothic

> Module guide for the **gothic** exterior style. It loads into the system prompt **only
> when the user picks the Gothic exterior**. An exterior style is the house's OUTSIDE
> finish — cladding, trim, roof colour, window treatment — plus a couple of signature
> added volumes. Build it yourself with ordinary ops on top of `05-building-houses.md`.

## The look

A **dark manor**: blackened **dark-oak boarding** over **deepslate**, a **steep slate
roof**, soul-lit lanterns, and two signature volumes — a **pointed corner spire** rising
past the roofline and a **glass conservatory wing** (a side full of windows). Brooding,
vertical, asymmetric.

## Materials (skin)

- **Walls:** `dark_oak_planks` framed by `dark_oak_log`, with `polished_blackstone` /
  `cobbled_deepslate` accents and footings.
- **Roof:** `deepslate_tile_stairs` (near-black slate), **steep** — gothic roofs are tall
  and sharp, with a **1-block overhang**.
- **Windows:** tall, narrow `glass_pane` lancets with `dark_oak` mullions; the
  conservatory uses `gray_stained_glass`.
- **Door / rails:** `dark_oak_door`, `dark_oak_fence`.
- **Light:** `soul_lantern` (cold blue) for the eerie cast.

## Signature volumes

1. **Corner spire / tower.** Raise a slender **square turret at one corner** that rises
   **above the main roofline**, capped by a **steep pointed (stepped-stairs) spire** with
   a fence finial. Pierce it with tall narrow window slits. This vertical accent is the
   gothic signature — make it clearly taller than the house.
2. **Glass conservatory wing.** Make **one side** of the house a **wall of windows** —
   re-glaze a side bay in `gray_stained_glass` framed by blackstone mullions (a sunroom /
   orangery lean reading as a "side full of glass"), optionally as a slightly projecting
   single-storey wing.
3. **Steep dark roof** with pronounced gables and overhangs.

## Avoid

- A symmetric, squat box — gothic is **tall, sharp and asymmetric**; the **spire** and
  the **glass side** must both be present.
- Warm/light materials or a shallow roof — keep everything **dark** and the pitch **steep**.
