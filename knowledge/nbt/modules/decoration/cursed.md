# Decoration module — Cursed (dark-stone gothic ruin)

> Loads when the user picks the **Cursed** decoration. It sets the MATERIALS + mood, not
> the massing — the structure type owns the shape. Cursed is the STONE counterpart to
> Haunted: where Haunted is a derelict timber house, Cursed is a derelict blackstone
> monolith (the look of the spire's references).

## The palette

- **Walls / ceilings**: `polished_blackstone_bricks`, weathering to
  `cracked_polished_blackstone_bricks` on decay patches.
- **Buttresses / ribs / piers**: `polished_blackstone` (a flat-black contrast to the brick).
- **Belt courses, jambs, brow detailing**: `chiseled_polished_blackstone`.
- **Footings / foundation**: `cobblestone`, weathering to `mossy_cobblestone`.
- **Trim / slabs / teeth**: `polished_blackstone_brick_slab`.
- **Stair core + stone caps**: `polished_blackstone_brick_stairs` (stays stone — never wood).
- **Glass**: grimy `gray_stained_glass` (solid sockets / sigils) + `gray_stained_glass_pane`
  (window slits). Never clear glass.
- **Light**: `soul_lantern` — the cold blue flame is the signature. Use soul fire, soul
  campfires and soul torches for accent light; keep warm light out.
- **Door / chains / finials**: `dark_oak_door`, `dark_oak_fence`.

## The mood

Heavily ruined by default (decay ~0.3): cracked stone, moss creeping up the footings,
chipped merlons, the odd missing block. Cold and tomb-like. Furnish the interior with
cobwebs, skulls, candles, soul campfires/braziers, tattered banners, chains, bone blocks and
the occasional brewing stand or enchanting table for a sorcerer's spire. Keep the whole
interior dim and blue-lit — the dread comes from the cold light and the cracked black stone.

## Pairs well with

The **Spire** (auto-selected together) for a cursed blackstone spire. Also strong on
a **Crypt** or **Cult temple** basement, or any keep you want to read as an abandoned, cursed
stone sanctum rather than a kept castle.
